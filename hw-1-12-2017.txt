5. The command ls -R list the contents of directories recursively. The command ls -t lists things by time of last change, with most recently changed files or directories first. In what order does ls -R -t display things?
	a. The directories are listed alphabetically at each level, the files/directories in each 	     directory are sorted by time of last change.
